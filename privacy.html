Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

This privacy declaration documentation rules the processing guidelines of the mobile system utility "Fishing Ping & Speed Test" created by independent developer STEVEN HART.

1. Zero Logs Architecture

Our commitment to user security means that we do not collect, capture, store, cache, log, or index any personal parameters, geographical location metadata, network diagnostics results, or hardware identifiers.

2. Dynamic Calculation Infrastructure

All networking requests, ping requests, and latency calculation metrics run strictly locally inside the active software layer on your phone. No background remote database transfers are configured.

3. Third-Party Policy

Because there are zero cloud databases generated on our side, your internal execution states are entirely private and are never distributed, sold, or reviewed by third-party advertising or telemetry bureaus.
